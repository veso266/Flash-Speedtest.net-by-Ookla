Speedtest Custom Overview

Karthik - November 08, 2017 23:20

Speedtest Custom is a robust and accurate testing solution that is HTML5-based, Flash-free and supports both mobile and desktop browsers. Speedtest Custom will enable you to test download and upload speeds, latency and jitter.

 

Speedtest Custom is:

  • Customizable: Simple tools allow you to customize your test to be consistent with your brand's look and feel. Use a customizable template or upload your own CSS. Update your test’s appearance yourself at any time via your account, and your updates will take effect immediately.
  • Global yet local: Leverage the Speedtest Server Network, making thousands of servers around the globe available for your test takers, or use your own server network.
  • Flexible access: Embed your test on your web page(s) via an iframe or direct customers to a unique Speedtest Custom subdomain.
  • Data-centric: View and download advanced test result analytics.
  • Responsive: Speedtest Custom can be used on internet-enabled devices of all shapes and sizes.
  • Shareable: Easy social buttons allow end users to share test results with other users.

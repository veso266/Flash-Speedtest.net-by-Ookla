How do I determine the version of my NetGauge client?

Team Ookla - November 11, 2016 22:34

To determine the version of your NetGauge client:

  1. Visit your instance of NetGauge in a browser
  2. Right-click on the application (within the netgauge.swf itself)
  3. A menu will appear displaying the version on the first line
  4. A build date is listed below the version for better tracking.

NetGauge: Client Configuration Templates

Team Ookla - November 11, 2016 19:21

NetGauge comes with a set of configuration templates to allow for quick setup based on the technology types and speeds being tested. The included templates are broadband, fiber, lan, gigabit.

To implement, simply insert the following line into your settings.xml:

<configuration connection="broadband" />

For details regarding the settings for each configuration template, reference the table below:

Templates can also be customized by simply adding the desired parameter(s) into your settings.xml. In the example below Netgauge will utilize the broadband template except for the download test length:

<configuration connection="broadband"/>
<socket-download testlength="20" />

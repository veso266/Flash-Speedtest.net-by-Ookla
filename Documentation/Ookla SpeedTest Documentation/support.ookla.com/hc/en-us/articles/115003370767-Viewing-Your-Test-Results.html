Viewing Your Test Results

Karthik - August 25, 2017 22:17

Limited Subscription

Your Speedtest Custom account gives you access to all test results conducted via your test for the current and previous month. You will be able to view a summary of test results for the past 30 days and download individual test results for the current and previous month. To access your results:

  1. Log in to your account at account.speedtestcustom.com/login.
  2. View a summary of test results for the past 30 days at the top of the Dashboard page.
  3. Click the Download Test Results CSV link in the middle of the Dashboard page to download individual test results for the current and previous month.

 

Premium Subscription

Your Speedtest Custom account gives you access to all historical test results conducted via your test. You will be able to view a summary of test results for the past 30 days, download individual test results for up to the last 10,000 tests, search for individual test results, and filter and analyze aggregated test result metrics. To access your results:

  1. Log in to your account at account.speedtestcustom.com/login.
  2. View a summary of test results for the past 30 days at the top of the Dashboard page.
  3. Click the Download Test Results CSV link in the middle of the Dashboard page to download individual test results. The csv export will include up to 10,000 of the most recent test results. If you need to view prior results, you may follow steps 4-7 to view and export the specific results you are for which you are looking.
  4. Select the Reporting link in the top right corner.
  5. Enter search or filtering criteria:
    1. Enter a city, region, or country in the search box at the top to filter results by location.
    2. Enter a result ID in the search box at the top to find a specific test result.
    3. Select a date range in the datepicker to filter results by date.
  6. View aggregated result metrics in the chart. You can toggle between download, upload, latency, test count, sample count and user count by clicking on each metric above the chart.
  7. View individual test results that match or search or filtering criteria.
  8. Click Export at the top to download individual results that match or search or filtering criteria.

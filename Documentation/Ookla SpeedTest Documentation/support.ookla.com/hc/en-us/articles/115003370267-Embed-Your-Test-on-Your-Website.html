Embed Your Test on Your Website

Karthik - December 11, 2017 04:12

With Speedtest Custom, you can choose to embed your customized test on your own web page rather than directing users to the Ookla-hosted page. If you would like to embed your test, follow these steps to add your website as an authorized Embed Test URL:

  1. Log in to your account at account.speedtestcustom.com/login.
  2. Select the Modify Configuration link in the middle of the page.
  3. Select the Embed tab.
  4. In the Embedded Test URLs box, enter the domain(s) where you will embed the test. Click the Add button after entering each URL. You may add up to 200 URLs.
  5. If you would like to make your test accessible only from the embedded URLs, including those that are accessible only from within your network, select the "Restrict to Embedded URL(s)" checkbox.
  6. Select the “Save & Publish” button at the top.
  7. Copy the iframe embed code.
  8. Paste it into the source code of the page(s) where you are embedding the test.
    1. For the best results on mobile displays, you may add the following tags:
      1. In the <head> tag, add <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width,initial-scale=1,minimum-scale=1.0 maximum-scale=1.0" />
      2. The wrapping container of the iframe and the `body` tag should have `width: 100%.
    2. An HTML template used for hosting your STC test can be found below:
